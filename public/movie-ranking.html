My top 3 Movies of all-time

Tenet

This sci-fi action thriller centered around a secret agent, known as "The Protagonist," who is tasked with preventing World War III. It use the concept of time manipulation.

Inception

It is sci-fi heist film that explores the concept of shared dreaming and the manipulation of the subconscious.

Drive

Super beautiful film. Really artistic.
